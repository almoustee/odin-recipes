Guacamole

Bowl of guac for hot summer

Guacamole is an avocado-based dip or spread that originated in Mexico. It's typically made with mashed avocado and lime juice, then seasoned with salt and cilantro. Guacamole often contains tomatoes and onions. This guacamole recipe gets a tasty kick from cayenne and cilantro. You can serve it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes. Serve with homemade tortilla chips

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 Lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon Salt
  • Vegetables
  • Herbs and spices

Steps:

  1. Mash avocados, lime juice, and salt together in a medium bowl; mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper
  2. Serve immediately, or cover and refrigerate for 1 hour for improved flavor
